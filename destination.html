1. France

The "City of Light," Paris is the epitome of romance, art, and culinary excellence. It's home to iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum, charming cobblestone streets in Montmartre, and world-class patisseries on every corner.

Pricing: ₹13,000 to ₹22,000

Category: Romantic & Cultural Getaway

2. Japan

A captivating land where ancient tradition meets futuristic technology. Experience the serene beauty of Kyoto's temples, the vibrant energy of Tokyo's neon-lit streets, the solemn history of Hiroshima, and the breathtaking sight of cherry blossoms in spring.

Pricing: ₹11,000 to ₹20,000

Category: Cultural & Modern Exploration

3. India

A vast country of incredible diversity and vibrant contrasts. Discover the timeless beauty of the Taj Mahal, explore the majestic palaces of Rajasthan, cruise the tranquil backwaters of Kerala, and immerse yourself in the spiritual atmosphere of Varanasi.

Pricing: ₹3,500 to ₹8,000

Category: Vibrant & Spiritual Adventure
